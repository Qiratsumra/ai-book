# Preface: The Era of Physical AI

Welcome to the **Preface**. Before diving into the technical details of humanoid robotics, we must first establish the philosophical and technological context for this new field: the era of Physical AI.

## ðŸ§  Introduction to Embodied Intelligence

The central focus of this textbook is **Embodied Intelligence**.

* **Definition:** Embodied Intelligence (EI) is the principle that an intelligent agent's cognitive abilities are deeply influenced, and often defined, by its physical body, sensors, and the environment it occupies. For a robot, this means its intelligence is not just software running on a computer, but the integrated result of its perception, locomotion, and interaction capabilities.
* **The Paradigm Shift:** We are moving away from purely abstract, disembodied AI (like large language models) toward systems that can perform complex, physical tasks. The challenge is no longer just *understanding* the world, but *acting* within it safely and intelligently.
* **Humanoid Significance:** Humanoid robots represent the ultimate testbed for embodied intelligence because their form factor allows them to navigate and manipulate tools designed for the human world.
